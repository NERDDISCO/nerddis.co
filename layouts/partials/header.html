The Artist

During the time when Tim Pietrusky studied information science he really got into electronic music.

After 5 years blinded by the light, he started to work on web apps that can be used to run "live visual spectacular"-shows and interact with other awesome projects like fivetwelve and modV.

Tim collaborates with various artists as either NERDDISCO or as digital artist of LiveJS or as the visual half of schickmalzwo.

The Organisation

At NERDDISCO we want to empower everyone to run their own "live visual spectacular"-shows by using our open source projects like:

  • luminave & luminave-thorium
  • WebUSB DMX512 Controller
  • NerdDrum

We also have a "Visual and Light"-workshop for any skill level, even remote!
